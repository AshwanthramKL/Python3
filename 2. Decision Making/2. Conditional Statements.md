# **Conditional Statements :**  

* ### **If Statement :**
    Checks for a condition.  
    *Syntax:*  
    ```python
    if condition:
        Statements
        .
        .
        .
    ```  
___

* ### **Else Statement :**
    Executes as alternate when the given condition is `False`.  
    *Syntax:*
    ```python
    if condition:
        Statements
        .
        .
        .

    else:
        Statements
        .
        .
        .
    ```

___


* ### **Elif Statement :**  
    Elif -> `Else if`  
    *Syntax:*
    ```python:
    if condition:
        Statements
            .
            .
            .

    elif condition:
        Statements
            .
            .
            .

    else:
        Statements
            .
            .
            .
    ```
